Alice A.I Discord App

Alice is a AI Discord chat-bot and image tool.
Alice can do many things such as a text to image interaction, image to image function, it can do upscaling.
And it includes many more features that it brings with its commands and interactions.
It has an LLM chat function, there are many ways to interact with the app.
You may direct message the bot, use /respond and or /describe
finally you can even @mention in a channel server channel given that it is permitted
(You may add permitted channels with /addchannel and the bot must be in the server to respond to pings)
