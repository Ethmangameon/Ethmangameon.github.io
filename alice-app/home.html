Alice A.I Discord app

Alice is a AI Discord chat-bot.
Alice can do many fun things such as a text to image interaction, image to image function, upscaling
with even more features that it brings with its commands and interactions.
It also has an LLM chat-bot, that you may direct message the app,
use /respond and or /describe and finally you can @mention in an channel that it is permitted
(You may add permitted channels with /addchannel)
